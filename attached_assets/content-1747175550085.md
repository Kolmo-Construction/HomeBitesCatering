## The Importance of Transparency at Home Bites Catering

- Clear Pricing: No hidden fees! Our transparent pricing helps you plan your budget with confidence. Instead of a traditional catering pricing sheet, we offer a catering pricing calculator that gives you a very good idea of our costs. When you request a quote, we will furnish detailed catering pricing to ensure you know exactly what to expect for your event.

- Menu Clarity: Our menu options are explained in detail, including ingredients, portion sizes, and any substitutions or limitations, ensuring you make informed choices.

- Service Details: We’re transparent about what’s included in our services—whether it’s staff, equipment, or additional services like setup and cleanup—so there are no surprises.

- Policies: We clearly communicate our policies regarding changes, cancellations, and special requests, giving you peace of mind that your needs will be met.

- Allergen & Dietary Information: We openly share how we accommodate dietary restrictions and allergens, so every guest feels safe and satisfied.
