Integrate/Replace Old Fields: Look at the existing beverage fields in EventInquiryFormData (like beverageServiceType, bartendingServiceType, servingAlcohol, nonAlcoholicBeverages from the original file). Decide if these new fields replace them or if the old ones can be incorporated. For example, the new alcoholicBeverageSelections.bartendingServiceType directly replaces the old bartendingServiceType. The old nonAlcoholicBeverages: Record<string, string> is less structured than the new nonAlcoholicBeverageSelections.
