Concept of the Custom Menu:
Instead of a fixed package, the Custom Menu option lets you build your own menu by selecting items from different cuisine styles or themes offered by Home Bites. The pricing appears to be determined per person based on selections (Custom Menu PP field) leading to a Total Custom Menu cost.

How it Works:

You first indicate you want a "Custom Menu".
You are then prompted to choose which food category you'd like to select from first.
After making selections from one category, you can choose to add items from another category or indicate that you are finished with your choices.
